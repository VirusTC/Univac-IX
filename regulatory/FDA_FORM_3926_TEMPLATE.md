# FDA Form 3926 Completion Guide for Treating Physicians

This template provides specialized instructions for hospital legal counsel and attending physicians completing **FDA Form 3926** (Individual Patient Expanded Access Investigational New Drug Application).

## Critical Field Guidance

### Field 8: Clinical History & Proposed Treatment Plan
*   **Required Verbiage:** "The patient presents with life-threatening [advanced oncology/severe microvascular trauma] complications. Standard available therapies are exhausted or clinically contraindicated. We propose the bedside administration of [SFWB / Bio-Synthetic Marrow] as an emergency, plant-derived fluid volume and metabolic support bridge under strict individual compassionate care guidelines."

### Field 9: Investigational Product Sourcing Identification
*   **Product Name:** Synthetic Fresh Whole Blood (SFWB) / Bio-Synthetic Marrow Complex
*   **Manufacturer:** [Insert Facility Name / Univac Processing Node Identifier]
*   **Reference Application:** [Insert Active Corporate IND Number provided in the Fox Rothschild briefing package].

### Field 10b: Request for Alternative IRB Review Pathway
*   **Action:** **Check "YES".**
*   **Legal Rationale:** Checking "YES" requests a waiver from the traditional requirement for a convened full-board IRB meeting. This allows treatment to safely begin immediately upon receiving written concurrence from the IRB Chairperson or a single designated board member.
