# Upstream Supply Chain: Certified Organic Farming & Raw Material Sourcing

This document defines the strict agricultural requirements for harvesting non-synthetic, organic inputs used in the production of Synthetic Fresh Whole Blood (SFWB).

## 1. Agricultural Inputs & Sourcing Matrices
Every raw ingredient must be tracked with a geographic coordinate identifier and batch timestamp to satisfy the Univac-IX blockchain ledger rules.

### Phase 1: High-Oleic Sunflower & Soy Core
*   **Farming Standard:** USDA Organic or European Union Organic certified soil profiles (monitored via satellite telemetry to ensure zero synthetic pesticide drifts).
*   **Harvesting Protocol:** Seeds must be cold-pressed under a dry nitrogen gas blanket at temperatures strictly $< 25^\circ\text{C}$ to prevent premature lipid peroxidation.

### Phase 2: Apis Mellifera Cappings Wax
*   **Sourcing Boundary:** Wild or organically managed apiaries located a minimum of 5 kilometers away from any commercial agricultural zones using synthetic chemicals.
*   **Processing:** Honey cappings must be removed mechanically and washed with sterile deionized water ($dH_2O$) to eliminate residual carbohydrate matrices before moving to the saponification loop.

### Phase 3: Root-Nodule Soy Leghemoglobin & Carotene
*   **Farming Standard:** High-density, non-GMO legume crops optimized for high root-nodule mass. 
*   **Extraction Window:** Nodules must be harvested mid-growth cycle when leghemoglobin concentration peaks, processed immediately under anaerobic conditions to maintain the functional iron-binding pocket.

## 2. Dynamic Batch-Yield Formula
The volume of raw organic inputs ($V_{raw}$) required to synthesize a standard 1,000 Liter batch of SFWB is determined by extraction efficiency coefficients:

$$V_{raw} = \sum \left( \frac{M_{target}}{\rho \cdot \eta_{extraction}} \right)$$

Where:
*   $M_{target}$ = Mass of the purified active component needed (e.g., 45,000g for albumin).
*   $\rho$ = Native density of the raw plant fluid matrix.
*   $\eta_{extraction}$ = Real-time agricultural yield efficiency index (updated hourly in the Univac system).
