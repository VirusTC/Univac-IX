### How Our Univac Plug-and-Play Strategy Works:

1. **Physical Interfacing:** Our proprietary hardware bridges are engineered to accept authentic Univac I/O connectors, spoolers, and magnetic readers. Simply plug the legacy drive into our modern matrix.
